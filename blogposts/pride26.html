But anyway, let's get started with the meat of things!

Why's this Pride month special?

This section will be very personal but bare with me, a lot has happened in my life leading up to this month, most major one being that I finally got my own bedroom and with that, actual privacy after years of living together in a very small room with 2 family members. The previous few years, which was almost half my teenagehood, was spent full of anxiety and me staying closed off from the things I actually liked doing, with very rare exceptions, which I still kept to myself. Other bullshit also went down but I'd rather not talk about that here. TO GET TO THE POINT the past few months, since I was given personal space, I've started to loosen up with it. And I can safely say I feel happier and a lot more confident in showing who I truly am to you guys aswell.

Not like I was hiding anything nefarious I was just not that confident about the things I like until now ykwim?

I also think this year's pride month is important due to that cultural step back that slowly happened. After that ~2020 period where people became supportive of queer culture a lot more because it was "trendy", things just went downhill. A lot of us found out about our identity due to this I think, but others just switched back to everything-phobia/ism as the initial peak dropped down. A lot of the world just sucks more now too! What I'm trying to get at here is that, following smaller patterns of queer culture being recognized trough, for example the game Tomodachi Life:Living the Dream recently, we are in an era where standing up for ourselves is more needed than ever, and there's starting to be demand for it on a larger scale yet again.

Governments are getting more comfortable being corrupt, people are more miserable than ever due to masking being normalized with social media's cringe and lolcow culture, wars... There just needs to be some positive openness out there again and after I had years to think about it, I think I'm ready to be more honest to myself and not hide that shit anymore.

Which lead me to this moment, where I reveal some more personal but really fun surprises I'm preparing and putting out throughout this month!
