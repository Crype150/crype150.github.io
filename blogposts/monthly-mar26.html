BUT THE GAME IS TOMODACHI LIFE: LIVING THE DREAM OH MY GOD NINTENDO DROP IT EARLY PLEASEEEE IT LOOKS SO COOL AGHH

Source: Tomodachi Life: Living the Dream Direct 29/01/2026 (YouTube)

Ahem... If anyone's wondering why this game is a big deal, think of The Sims and an ant farm combined. You are watching a town of Mii characters you create live their lives on their own accord, while you help them with various problems they have, and costumize their living space by buying decorations, interiors, drawing them shit, hell add the cherry on top, draw on THEM!

Source: Tomodachi Life: Living the Dream Direct 29/01/2026 (YouTube)

You can make custom clothing, props, toys, food, basically a sitcom drama made into a game, where you can have maximum 70 characters simultaneously doing dumb shit, falling in love, experiencing a breakup etc. etc. etc.

This game is especially making the rounds online by the sheer amount of diversity it has compared to a lot of simulation games similar to it and the complete lack of profanity filters on what you can tell the Mii characters to say (in the demo Nintendo released). It's really, really funny and I'm gonna sink so much time into it making my OCs make out passionately while chatting about, qoute "Jiggling bellies."
