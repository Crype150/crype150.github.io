The Crype.xyz debut

2026/01/2
Announcement!!!

It's finally fucking here

Well here we are, I finally launched my own personal website for everyone to see. One of my biggest projects so far which can also help aid my future projects. What a way to start 2026 huh?

But seriously, I am so glad I was able to finish it in time, this is huge in many ways to me, especially as an outlet to whatever I feel like saying and putting out into the world. And of course, this wouldn't have been able to exist without the support of my community (and you, who's reading this)!

I got a lot to more to say about the future of this place and I will also have to write another blog post going over changes with me as a whole this year, so let's just get to the meat of things already.

It's just a Beta right now?

Yes! I currently label the state of the website as a public beta due to some features that I still wish to add but didn't allocate enough time to finish for the publishing.

Also... bugs... and mobile compatibility...

Speaking of the extremely conveniently portable devil...

Be warned! Some things may look a little broken on mobile! Although I did try my absolute best to make the website work for that aspect ratio, every function should still be usable regardless of the broken visual stuff. I would love to fix all of it, but trust me I tried, and I will further try in the future, I just really don't feel like delaying the publishing further.

The website might still have some oversights even after me thoroughly going over everything, so if you find any let me know!

Why is this website worth my time?

Well that's really for you to decide, the reason I made this was mainly for myself to more easily organize my work and have a good place to pour my heart into, because holy fuck I got a lot to say all the time and I am sooooooooooooooo tired of keeping it to myself, I've grown out of that quiet puppy phase a while ago so I just wanna be more myself now.

If you want a start to look for a reason to come here, I highly recommend the Gallery! Some of my highest quality work is presented there with a great preview feature to see it better. I'm proud of each and every piece I put on there and I hope it will make you smile too!

You can also look at my OC Gallery (even if there's not much there yet) or sign my GuestBook! You can ask a question there and I can directly answer you, even if you want to stay anonymous, with both your name and message. :]
(I probably have to email you if you send a private message though, just keep that in mind)
