Crype (or Crype150) is the main mascot of my online presence who I represent myself with the most publicly. The character represents an anthropomorphic Creeper with glowing yellow eyes. They do not represent themselves with a sexuality or gender and they go by any pronouns. Crype has different versions depending on what universe I feature them in, information about those versions will be available on here in the future.

Crype's name originates from an old account called "Creepe150" I made for a Minecraft server that required it's own launcher and account system to bypass the Minecraft EULA (I will not name said server for my own legal safety).

Their design went trough various changes overtime before it ended up the way it is today.
