Tryax (or... uh... Tryax Tryax... I'll explain later) is part of what they call the Modern Tyrannosaurus Rex species, a variant of which that live on the warmer climates, are colored with orange-y hues and have feather-like hair (often Green, like his). Tryax goes by he/him pronouns and he's the groundskeeper at Cyren's and Derekk's apartment building while also running the jewelry next to it as one of the fronts for their criminal group with Cy and Greg.

He's very open and confident about being a sassy jerk but if he finds common ground with someone he only shows how much he actually cares indirectly, with sticky note messages and gifts he never delivers himself.

His full name is kind of silly, and no it's not just me not being able to come up with a proper name for him. He basically just couldn't come up with a good name himself. Yeah blah blah blah trans stereotype yada yada yada I just think it's funny and honestly never even thought about that before I saw a twitter post about it yet again. Anyway, why couldn't he just keep his normal name? Well he just wasn't comfortable with it after cutting his parents off, since they hardly ever respected his identity and couldn't even fathom why he secretly transitioned while away for university. He never gets into the weeds of it, or if he was even fair with his parents regarding this, but he does tell this to Cyren at some point before they stop being very close friends.

Oh yeah he has a very deep few year history with Cyren, they had a weird situationship at some point and Tryax always loved pushing how transparent Cyren's closet is, if you know what I mean. After Derekk moved in with Cyren he suddenly stopped messing with him that hard, because he saw that the red dragon had feeling for him (and also Greg gossiped about his and Cy's interactions).
