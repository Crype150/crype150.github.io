Rechie (or Rechin Rebal) is a lemon shark inspired lemon colored shark. He's Greg's roommate, the slightly older one of the duo, openly homosexual and goes by he/him pronouns. He originally grew up in a wealthy family, but due to a lot of disagreements with his parents after years of not actually being respected for who he is, he silently left and changed his identity. Due to this and because he doesn't want to be found by his family again, he can't really have very well paying jobs, so he works at a beachside restaurant with a very questionable dresscode...

Other than his unusual job as a waiter on a beach, he does professional e-sports in various shooters and strategy games for fun and as a way to make some extra money. He allows himself to be known in the gamer niche because he knows his parents are extremely out of touch tone deaf boomers who are psychologically stuck in the last century still. While Greg beats Rechie in height, Rechie is top 100 on the global Fortnite leaderboard* so it doesn't matter, he's still cooler. And Rechie won at least 25.000 Lios** in competitions (That's at least 2 months of rent!)

As much as he sounds dumb and lazy that's mostly a coverup. He graduated one of the best schools in the world but he always felt like it was way too much weight on his back. He learns really fast but the amount of subjects he was pressured to master overwhelmed him, which came with the realization that he would be way happier if he was dumber.
