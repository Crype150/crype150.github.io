Derekk (or Drekkan Orvell) is a very rare ice dragon species, mostly found in the savanna or snow-land areas of the city (Zootopia style) and goes by he/they pronouns. He's a university student, working for an IT degree while doing most of the chores around the apartment he lives in with Cyren. He struggles a lot with being clumsy due to his small size, because he's just full of fur everywhere. Thankfully though, he doesn't boil under there due to being cold blooded, which neutralizes the high temperature he would experience otherwise. He also looks very funny when drying up, look

Derekk is the youngest and probably the most average guy in the cast. He's kind of a perfectionist though, which causes some complications when it comes to fairly simple mistakes. Cyren thinks it's kind of cute though, Derekk silently enjoys that. Yes, he has a crush on Cyren. He wishes he could tell him but that bundle of meat doesn't know shit about romance...

Derekk eventually finds out about Cyren and Greg's little arrangement with that criminal gang and become nosy about it to the point he starts helping out with some of the hacker skills he jokingly learned throughout high school in his free time. For some odd reason he knows a lot about breaking into, like, a lot of things aswell, I wonder what that hacking magic was used for before he knew about the gang.
